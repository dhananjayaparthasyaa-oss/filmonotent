REVIEW - THE BADASS OF BOLLYWOOD

The Badass of Bollywood is a Netflix series that marks the directorial debut of Shah Rukh Khan’s son, Aryan Khan—and honestly, it looks pretty solid.

Performance-wise, the show shines the most. Manoj Pahwa and Raghav Juyal completely steal the spotlight with their impeccable comic timing.

The lead, Lakshya Lalwani, also delivers well. His chemistry with Raghav feels natural. Bobby Deol as the antagonist finally gets a role that allows him to show his acting chops.

The series features multiple cameos—some land well, some don’t—but they add variety.

Comedy is definitely the highlight here. Most of the jokes land, and even the situational comedy works effectively.

The downside, however, is the VFX—it’s a huge letdown. Also, the frequent use of abusive language feels a bit overdone.

Overall: The Badass of Bollywood is fun, sharp, and entertaining. Despite its flaws, it’s a refreshing watch that deserves a chance. Definitely give it a try!
